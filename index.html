Questions? Answered.

Do I need to know music theory?

Nope. Sonorus handles key selection, chord voicing, and harmonic function for you. Just describe what you want - "sad verse", "upbeat chorus" - and it figures out the theory. That said, if you do know theory, the chord picker gives you full control over every harmonic choice.

What DAWs does the MIDI export work with?

All of them. The export is a standard MIDI file. Ableton Live, Logic Pro, FL Studio, GarageBand, Reaper, Pro Tools, Cubase - if it reads MIDI, it works.

Is Sonorus free?

Yes. Sonorus is free during beta. Just open it and start writing.

How is this different from other AI music tools?

Most AI music tools generate audio clips you can't edit. Sonorus generates chord progressions - structured, editable, exportable as MIDI. You keep full control over every note, and you finish the production in your own DAW with your own sounds.

Can I edit the chords after generating them?

Absolutely - that's the point. Use the AI to describe changes ("make the bridge darker"), swap chords manually from the harmonic picker, or do both. Every chord in every section is fully editable.

Can I write lyrics in Sonorus?

Yes. Each section has a lyrics panel so your words and chords live in the same place. Write while the harmony is fresh, then export the whole thing when you're ready.
